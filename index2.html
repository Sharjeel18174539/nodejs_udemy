Non Vegetarian List

Non-vegetarian food (in Indian English sometimes shortened to non-veg food)
contains meat (red meat, poultry, seafood, or the flesh of any other animal),
and sometimes, eggs. The term is common in India, but not usual elsewhere.

chicken butter :
It is a type of curry made from chicken with a spiced tomato and butter (makhan) sauce. Its sauce is known for its rich texture. It is similar to chicken tikka.

chicken Masala :
Chicken Masala is a North Indian dish where chicken is cooked in a spicy onion tomato masala. A basic masala is prepared by saut√©ing onions, tomatoes, spices and herbs.

Fish Fry :
A fish fry is a meal containing battered or breaded fried fish. It usually also includes french fries, coleslaw, macaroni salad, lemon slices, tartar sauce, hot sauce, malt vinegar and dessert.

Mutton korma :
A dish with its origin in the Indian subcontinent, consisting of meat or vegetables braised with yogurt, water or stock, and spices to produce a thick sauce or gravy.
